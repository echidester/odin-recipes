Simmer Sauce Butter Chicken

Only have a few minutes to throw together dinner? Try this delicious butter chicken recipe that is incredibly hands-off!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • Maya Kaimal Butter Chicken Simmer Sauce
  • 1 cup uncooked basmati rice

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, add uncooked rice and 1.5 cups water over medium-high heat. Once water begins to boil, reduce heat to medium-low and cover saucepan for ~15 minutes while rice cooks.
  2. In a large pan, cook cook chicken over medium-high heat until no longer pink, 5-7 minutes.
  3. Once chicken is no longer pink, add jar of simmer sauce to the pan. Continue to cook until the sauce has reduced, 10-15 minutes.
