White Chicken Chili

Comfort in a bowl. Make it as spicy as you want by grabbing spicier green salsa!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 1 jar salsa verde
  • 2 cans white beans
  • 4-5 carrot sticks, sliced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 carton chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 0.5 tbsp paprika
  • 0.5 tbsp salt

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook cook chicken over medium-high heat until no longer pink, 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add all other ingredients to the pot. Turn heat down to simmer.
  3. Simmer for 20-60 minutes depending on your consistency preferences.
