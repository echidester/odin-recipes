Easy Fajitas

These fajitas are the best recipe I've found anywhere! It's easy and absolutely delicious.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons canola oil, divided
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1.5 teaspoons seasoned salt
  • 1.5 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1.5 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon chili powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, optional
  • 1.5 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 0.5 medium sweet red pepper, sliced
  • 0.5 medium green pepper, sliced
  • 0.5 medium onion, sliced
  • 6 flour tortillas, warmed
  • Toppings! Ideas include shredded cheddar cheese, taco sauce, salsa, guacamole, sliced red onions, and sour cream.

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine 2 tablespoons oil, lemon juice and seasonings; add the chicken. Turn to coat; cover. Refrigerate for 1-4 hours.
  2. In a large cast-iron or other heavy skillet, saute peppers and onions in remaining oil until crisp-tender. Remove and keep warm.
  3. Drain chicken, discarding marinade. In the same skillet, cook chicken over medium-high heat until no longer pink, 5-6 minutes. Return pepper mixture to pan; heat through.
  4. Spoon filling down the center of tortillas. Add toppings as desired, fold in half.
